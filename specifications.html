Installation Specifications

YourWall panels are designed for rapid and efficient installation, requiring minimal specialized tools and skills. The modular system uses a track-and-panel approach that delivers consistent results.

Track System

Component Specification
Floor Track Galvanized steel, 1.5mm thick, pre-drilled at 400mm centers
Ceiling Track Galvanized steel, 1.5mm thick, pre-drilled at 400mm centers
Wall Tracks Galvanized steel, 1.5mm thick, pre-drilled at 400mm centers
Fixings 8mm expansion bolts for concrete, appropriate fixings for other substrates

Panel Connections

Connection Type Details
Panel to Panel Tongue and groove interlocking system with cam-lock engagement
Panel to Track Friction fit with securing brackets at 600mm centers
Corner Joints Custom corner profiles with interlocking connections
Joint Treatment Silicone sealant for acoustic seal, no tape or compound required

All YourWall products undergo rigorous quality control procedures throughout the manufacturing process:

  • 100% raw material inspection
  • In-process testing at multiple stages
  • Dimensional verification of all components
  • Final assembly inspection
  • Batch testing for thermal and acoustic performance
  • Random sampling for destructive testing

Test reports and certificates are available upon request for specific project requirements.
