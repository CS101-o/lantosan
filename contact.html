FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How quickly can you deliver wall panels for my project?

Standard lead times are 4-6 weeks from order confirmation, but this can vary based on project scale and current production capacity. For urgent projects, we offer expedited manufacturing options where possible.

Do you provide installation services?

While we don't directly provide installation services, we work with a network of certified installers across the country who are fully trained in our systems. We can recommend qualified installers in your area.

Can I customize the dimensions of the panels?

Yes, we offer custom manufacturing to meet specific project requirements. Our standard sizes are most economical, but we can produce panels in a wide range of dimensions to suit your needs.

What information do you need for a quote?

For an accurate quote, we typically need project location, panel quantities and dimensions, performance requirements (acoustic, thermal, fire), and delivery timeframe. Plans or drawings are helpful but not essential for initial quotes.
