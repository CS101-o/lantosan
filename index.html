WHAT IS YOURWALL?

YourWall is an advanced interior and exterior wall system with independent performance data for:

  • Thermal insulation
  • Airborne sound insulation
  • Reaction to fire
  • Fire resistance
  • Robustness

Test data confirming the product performance undertaken at accredited test facilities is available.

Although YourWall is water permeable it can be installed where water ingress is still present because it's not impacted by the water and it dries out.

YourWall is a modular off site manufactured walling system that is fast to install, and ready to decorate without the need of a plaster finish.

FACTORY MADE

Our panels are manufactured in a controlled factory environment ensuring consistent quality and precision. This approach provides numerous benefits:

  • Higher quality control standards

  • Weather-independent manufacturing
  • Customizable to project requirements
