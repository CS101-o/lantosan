Our Story

Rooted in a legacy dating back to 1969, Lentosan stands on the solid foundation of decades-long experience in the construction sector. While our official establishment as Lentosan Construction Technologies Inc. dates to 2018, our journey began much earlier under the name Yıldırım Construction Energy Informatics Industry and Trade Ltd., actively trading construction materials since 2007.

Driven by a deep commitment to innovation, reliability, and quality, we have grown to become a trusted name in providing advanced wall panel systems and construction solutions. Today, our products and services reach across Turkey/Anatolia, the Middle East, Europe, Gulf Region, and North Africa, contributing to a wide range of projects from residential buildings to industrial and infrastructure developments.

With every panel we produce, we build not only structures but also long-term partnerships grounded in trust, performance, and shared vision.
