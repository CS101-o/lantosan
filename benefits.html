TIME SAVING

YourWall Systems dramatically reduces construction time compared to traditional methods:

  • 55% reduction in labor hours for wall installation
  • No need for wet trades, eliminating drying times
  • Factory-made panels arrive ready to install
  • Simplified installation process requires minimal specialized skills
  • Parallel workflow capability for accelerated project timelines

Our time studies show that a typical room can be enclosed with our system in just 1-2 days, compared to 4-7 days using traditional methods. This time efficiency translates directly to cost savings and earlier project completion.

COST EFFICIENCY

YourWall Systems delivers significant cost benefits through multiple channels:

  • Reduced labor costs through faster installation times
  • Minimized material waste compared to traditional construction
  • No need for specialized plastering or finishing trades
  • Reduced on-site storage requirements
  • Lower project management overhead due to simplified process
  • Long-term savings through superior thermal performance

While the material cost may be comparable to traditional systems, the total installed cost is typically 15-25% lower when all factors are considered.
